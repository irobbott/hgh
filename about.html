    About Us

    Hope Grace Happiness Mobile Labs is a compassionate, client-centered mobile phlebotomy service committed to bringing quality care to your doorstep. We understand that lab work can be stressful, inconvenient, or even overwhelming—especially for seniors, children, and individuals with special needs. That's why we make it simple, safe, and stress-free by coming to you.

    Whether you're at home, work, or a care facility, our trained and certified professionals provide reliable, nearly painless blood draws with a gentle touch and full respect for your privacy. Every visit is handled with the highest standards of safety, professionalism, and kindness.
    We proudly serve individuals, families, and organizations that need flexible, on-site lab services—delivered with hope, grace, and happiness.

    About Jamie Glaspie

    Hi, I'm Jamie Glaspie. I've worked in phlebotomy for over 27 years, beginning in a hospital before transitioning to private practice and medical assisting. One day, I drew blood for the Dean of a college—and that opened the door for me to become a teacher. Since 2008, I've been a phlebotomy instructor and continue to share my love of this field with new students.

    I've also served as a supervisor and manager, and I'm a certified BLS (Basic Life Support) Instructor. Whether I'm leading a team, teaching, or drawing blood, my goal is the same: make each patient feel safe, seen, and as comfortable as possible. Many don't even realize I've completed the draw—it's an art I take pride in.

    Now, through my mobile phlebotomy service, I bring that same compassionate care to you—where you feel most at ease.

    Jamie Glaspie

    Founder | Certified Phlebotomist | Instructor | BLS Instructor
