    Hi, I'm Jamie Glaspie. I've worked in phlebotomy for over 27 years, beginning in a hospital before transitioning to private practice and medical assisting. One day, I drew blood for the Dean of a college—and that opened the door for me to become a teacher. Since 2008, I've been a phlebotomy instructor and continue to share my love of this field with new students.
